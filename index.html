About Marcus Lewis

Welcome to my Portfolio!

Education

Hello, my name is Marcus Lewis, and welcome to my Portfolio webpage, where you will find my most recent and what I consider my best works in regard to development. My education consists of a Bachelor's degree in English, received from the University of California, Irvine, and I have received a certificate noting my completion of the Full Stack Coding Boot Camp, also from the University of California, Irvine.

Work Experience

My work experience began in 2013, when I first landed an internship as a Programming Assistant at Lockheed Martin. Although short, I was able to grasp the basics of CATIA and C++. My line of work shifted toward laboratory work and technical media during my college years, and I have learned a number of different skills such as video editing, film production, and handling laboratory grade equipment. After my college years, and receiving my Bachelor's degree, I was able to land a position in the financial field, specifically an Underwriter position. I enjoyed learning about the ins and outs of how businesses operate, understanding cashflow statements, and exposing myself to a number of other financial markers that signal trustworthiness of an entity. After my experience as an Underwriter, I now work as a Content Analyst at Bitvore, and my work entails reviewing content pertaining to municipal bonds. I am very interested in the financial field, and would love to combine my knowledge of the financial world and development, as well as continue learning about both fields!
